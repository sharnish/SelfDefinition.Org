From Rose

1674 High St., Benwood, W.Va.

April 19, 1961

Friend Al,

Received your letter. Ill when I wrote the last one. Worse now. Will write when I get the fever out of my head and can think more rationally.

Sincerely,

Dick

[signature in longhand]
