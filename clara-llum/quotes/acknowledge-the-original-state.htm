realization is to understand who/what are you ~ realization is to distinguish between the illusory and the real

you are not body, mind, or person ~ you are the infinite one, the Self, the supreme and absolute

to understand means to be established at the root or ground of reality, not a mere logical or analytical apprehension

to realize you need to penetrate into the nonconceptual state which is your true nature ~ that is called contemplation or nondual meditation

complementary and preliminary to the primordial yoga of the natural state are the paths of faith and discernment

have faith in the teaching that places you before the transcendental truth: "you are That", together with the other great statements, making it yours

investigate the dream-like texture of the changing manifestation, experiencing it against the background of the immutable unmanifest, and understand it’s its expression

pull your awareness back from the character and drama, to the light projecting them: free from form and multiplicity ~ you are that light, the witness and creator

if you see me, you see your self

~ Clara Llum

Reconoce el Estado Original

realización es comprender quién/qué eres tú – realización es distinguir lo ilusorio de lo real

tú no eres cuerpo, mente, o persona – tú eres el infinito uno, el Ser, lo supremo y absoluto

comprender significa estar situado en la raíz o base de la realidad, no un entendimiento meramente lógico o analítico

para realizar debes penetrar en el estado no conceptual que es tu verdadera naturaleza – a esto se llama contemplación o meditación no dual

complementarias y preliminares al yoga primordial del estado natural son las vias de la fe y del discernimiento

ten fe en la enseñanza que te situa ante la verdad trascendente: "Tu eres Eso", junto a las otras grandes predicaciones, haciendola tuya

investiga la textura onírica de la cambiante manifestacion, contrastandola con lo inmutable inmanifestado, y entiende que es su expresion

remontate del personaje y del drama, a la luz que lo proyecta: carente de forma y de pluralidad ~ tu eres esa luz, el testigo y creador

si me ves a mi, te ves a ti

~ Clara Llum
