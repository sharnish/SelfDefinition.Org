Obeisances to the Supreme Guru, Sri Samarth Dattatreya Sai Nath of Shirdi.

May the Moon of the Intellect reflect the pure Light-Knowledge of the Sun-Self, by the Grace of the Guru who is One and the Very Same with the Heart, One’s Being-Awareness-Bliss.

Happy Guru Purnima Day!

~ Clara Llum
