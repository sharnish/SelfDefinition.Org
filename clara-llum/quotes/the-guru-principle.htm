people think they can adopt and then drop a guru

a guru is the one who introduces you to your true nature

your true nature and the guru are inseparable and the same

inseparable from each other, even if you leave her, she remains as you

given that she lives within as your true nature, in due time, due to the deepening into your true nature, you can’t help but examine your mind and actions about her

this inquiry will purify your mind from the false separation and its associated concepts

the guru-sishya relationship is indestructible, as is based in truth

it is the relationship of you with your Self

the guru is the spiritual mother who from this world-womb delivers you into the absolute beyond

~ Clara Llum
