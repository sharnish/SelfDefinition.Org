NAMASTE means 'not me but you'

"not me" means "I bow", "I surrender" (to you)

"me" and "I" refers to the ego/mind

"you" is the Self, the Divine, and any other One

to say and feel NAMASTE is sadhana, bhakti yoga and jnana yoga

is it too to say NAMO SHIVAYA,

NAMO BUDDHA,

NAMO AMITABHA

which mean ~ I surrender

to the Source of All Goodness,

to Awakened Awareness,

to Infinite Light

~ Clara Llum

Namaste

NAMASTE significa "no yo sino tu"

"no yo" significa "me inclino", "me rindo" (a ti)

"me" y "yo" se refiere al ego/mente

"ti" y "tu" es el Ser, lo Divino, y cada Uno

decir y sentir NAMASTE es sadhana, bhakti yoga y jnana yoga

tambien lo es NAMO … Shivaya, Buddha, Amitabha…

que significan respectivamente "me entrego a…"

la fuente de todo bien,

la conciencia despierta,

la luz infinita

~ Clara Llum
