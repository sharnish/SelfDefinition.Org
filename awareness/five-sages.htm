THE DIRECT PATH LINKS THE FIRST RING (most direct)

Sri Muruganar was a liberated sage who spent decades in the company of Sri Ramana Maharshi. Sri Muruganar was already a highly skilled poet before he met Sri Ramana Maharshi, and therefore his ability to communicate clearly is unsurpassed. One liberated sage reporting on the teachings of another liberated sage is like two mirrors facing each other. The result is an infinite reflection. Sri Muruganar's words are a living transmission. The teachings found in the First Ring links are the most clearly communicated, condensed summary of the teachings of the Direct Path that exist today. With orbits around the sun (the Self), the orbit closest to the sun is the first ring, and here on the Direct Path Links Directory, the most Direct Teachings that exist today, communicated most clearly, in the most concise manner are called "THE FIRST RING".

THE SECOND RING (second most direct)

Sri Sadhu Om spent five years in the company of Sri Ramana Maharshi and decades in the company of Sri Muruganar.

Sri Sadhu Om wrote a book called "The Path of Sri Ramana Part 1" which contains the most detailed teaching on the practice of Self-inquiry, Self-attention and Self-abidance that exists today. For those people who studied the Third Ring teachings and came to the mistaken conclusion that the Direct Path is Self-inquiry in the form of asking "To whom do these thoughts arise?" and "Who am I?" etc., Sri Sadhu Om provides the bridge leading one from the Third Ring Teachings to the First Ring Teachings. The reason Sri Sadhu Om's teachings have been placed under the Second Ring is because it takes Sri Sadhu Om 100 words to say what Sri Muruganar could say in only 10 words. Sri Sadhu Om's ability to communicate is very good, but not as skilled as Sri Muruganar.

However, the fact that he is the Second Ring and not third or fourth or fifth, is a way of praising the great valuable contribution that Sri Sadhu Om has made to the understanding of the Direct Path and what it means to inquire into, attend to and abide as the Self.

The second ring teaching is the book "The Path of Sri Ramana, Part 1". It is a detailed guide to the actual practice of Self-inquiry, Self-attention, Self-abidance. What Sri Sadhu Om reveals in these teachings is profound and extraordinary. Sri Sadhu Om is very creative in his ability to use examples and illustrations to answer those questions that almost no one has ever answered clearly. What Sri Sadhu Om reveals lays the foundation for an illuminated, clear understanding of the Direct Path and the Teachings of Sri Ramana Maharshi.

When a person is no longer interested in doing a lot of reading or thinking about, spiritual teachings, but they are only concerned with practice instructions and encouragement to practice, then the First Ring teachings are the best choice. However, if one is still interested in learning the details of the matrix in which the teachings arise, or interested in thinking about the teachings and would like to learn intellectually what inquiry, attention, abidance, and the Direct Path of Sri Ramana Maharshi mean, then the second ring teachings are the best choice for that.

The Second Ring teachings are also a step by step guide to the practice of Self-inquiry, Self-abidance and Self-attention.

People would visit Sri Sadhu Om and ask him various questions and tell him that they were not making any progress with asking 'To whom do these thoughts arise?', etc. Sri Sadhu Om's answer to such questions became the book "The Path Of Sri Ramana, Part One". What Sri Sadhu Om reveals about the practice of Self-inquiry in that book amounts to a person who was traveling in a Bullock Cart (asking 'to whom do these thoughts arise?') being introduced to Jet Travel (Awareness watching awareness).
