Tibetan Links adapted from: thangkagallery.org

Last update 1 June 2022

  • Tibet.Net - the new multilingual site of the Tibetan government in exile, contains the various information on Tibet and the Tibetan culture.
  • DharmaNet.Org International WebRing - well known common-buddhist site including a gateway to Buddhist resources on the Internet, and also the special search-machine.
  • Padmasambhava.Org Buddhist Center - Buddist center provided by Kenchen Palden Sherab and Khenpo Tsawang Dongyal rinpoche.
  • The Aro Tradition, aroter.org - Aro gTer tradition.
  • Rigpa.Org - centers of Sogyal Rinpoche and Dzogchen Rinpoche.
  • Nyingma.Org - centers of Tartang Tulku Rinpoche.
  • ChagdudGonpa.Org Foundation - the center for Chagdud Tulku Rinpoche.
  • Wikipedia entry for Chagud Tulku Rinpoche (1930–2002)
  • Shechen.Org / Shechen Tennyi Dargyeling Monastery - Shechen tradition of Dilgo Khyentse Rinpoche and Rabjam Rinpoche.
  • Palyul.Org / H.H. Drubwang Pema Norbu Rinpoche - Pema Norbu (Penor) Rinpoche.
  • TashiCholing.Org / Tashi Choling Center for Buddhist Studies - the center for Gyatrul Rinpoche (b. 1924)
  • Tersar.Org is now DudjomTersar.Org / Yeshe Nyingpo NYC - estab. by Dudjom Rinpoche (1904-87) - the site of Shenpen Dawa Rinpoche, son of Dudjom Rinpoche.
  • Dogchen.Org - the site of Dzogchen Monastery.
  • PatrulRinpoche.Org - the site of Dzogchen Ranyak Dza Patrul Rinpoche.
  • Gomde.Dk (Denmark) / Rangjung Yeshe Gomde - the site of Chokyi Nyima Rinpoche.
  • Shedrub.Org - Ka-Nying Shedrub Ling
  • Ligmincha.Org / The Ligmincha Institute - the biggest in the Western world center of Dzogchen in Bon tradition under leading of Tenzin Wangyal Rinpoche.
  • YungdrungBon.Com - YungdrungBon Study Centre, UK
  • FPMT.Org - The Foundation for the Preservation of the Mahayana Tradition, founded by Lama Sopa.
  • FPMT in Mexico
  • StudyBuddhism.Com / The Berzin Archives - new site, dedecated to works and translations of wrold-famous Buddhist scientist and translator dr.Alex Berzin.
  • HimalayanArt.Org - on-line gallery of thangkas and other artifacts of Tibetan art.
  • www.Khordong.de/Tsagli/ (broken link) - the private site of Wolfgang Zimmerman with great collection of traditionat Tibetan painting tsagli.
  • Drikung.Org - Official Website of the Drikung Kagyu
