Version comparison

English: Source, Buddhist Bible by Dwight Goddard, 1932 (copyright not renewed) based on the translation by Mr. Wong Mou-lam.

Link: https://www.sacred-texts.com/bud/bb/index.htm

To-Do Notes: The Spanish version has a much more lengthy preface. (Need to check the original for the English version.) The English version was 9 chapters and was extracted from The Buddhist Bible. The Spanish version was 10 chapters and was apparently based on a somewhat different source, although they are very similar. The Spanish version has been rearranged so that the chapters correspond to the English version. No material was excluded.
