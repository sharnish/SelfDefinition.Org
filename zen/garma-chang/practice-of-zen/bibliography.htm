Chu Hung. Ch'an Kuan Ts'e Chin (exhortations on advancement through the Ch'an Gates).

Hsu Yun. Hsu Yun Yu Lu (the discourse of Master Hsu Yun).

Hui Chiao. Kao Seng Chuan (the biographies of outstanding monks).

Hui Neng. Lu T'su Tan Chin (the Sutra of the Sixth Patriarch).
